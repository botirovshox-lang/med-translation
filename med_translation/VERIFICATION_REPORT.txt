SUPPORT REFERENCE
═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Common Questions:
Q: Where are my buttons?
A: Look in columns 12-13 of each segment row

Q: When should I click Confirm?
A: When translation is complete and QA passed

Q: What happens when I click Clear?
A: Translation is deleted, status reset to 'new'

Q: Is my translation saved?
A: Yes, always saved immediately after editing

Q: How does Confirm differ from Save?
A: Confirm adds to TM, Save is automatic on edit

For more, see RELEASE_NOTES.md section "Common Issues & Solutions"
